The Portfolio of Ben Fang

Project 1 Gold

Game

Controls:

Arrow keys to move

Z to jump

X to shoot

space to switch between missle and bullets(when applicable)

"1" for cheats

"4" for bonus level

Developer Commentary:

Hi, I am Ben one of the two developers of this Nes Metroid clone. I worked with Will Gushee, and we split up most of the tasks between us. Will worked on the enemies, sprites, audio, and refining the game. I focused on the health system, camera/screen transitions, and creating large modules for the game. In the end we were able to finish the original first level of Metroid and also make a custom level that you can access by pressing "4" while playing the game. 

I hope you will enjoy this game!

Project 2 Gold

Game

Controls:

A and D to Move

Mouse to aim

Left click to shoot and Space to pull

Shift to cut

Gamepad:

Left joystick to move Right joystick to aim

Right Trigger to shoot

Left Trigger to pull

bottom button to cut ropes

Developer Commentary:
